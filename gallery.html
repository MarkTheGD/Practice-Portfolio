Key Insights from My Journey

🧠 Learning by Doing

Reading about HTML is useful, but building actual projects creates lasting understanding. Each website I built taught me more than hours of theory.

🔍 Details Matter

Semantic markup, accessibility, and proper structure aren't optional extras - they're fundamental to professional web development.

🚀 Consistent Progress

Seven days of focused, daily practice created more progress than months of sporadic learning. Consistency beats intensity.

🤝 Community Support

Learning with AI assistance and connecting with the development community accelerated my understanding and provided valuable guidance.
